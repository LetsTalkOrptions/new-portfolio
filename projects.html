Projects

MovieBase

MovieBase is an interactive site that uses AJAX calls to pull movie data from different APIs while displaying some movie facts. Users have a creative, clean, simple design that is attractive and compatible on any device. Also included is a comments page where users could post reviews of movies and stored their responses in a Firebase.. I was responsible for the concept, UI, all design, media queries, and usage of API's.

All Aboard

All Aboard is a website that is an updated look of a traditional train schedule board. Users can input and add to the board while storing the data via Firebase, receiving updated times necessary to be on time for their train.

GIPH ME A FEELING

GIPH ME A FEELING is a simple site that allows users to type in feelings they have and get a list of giphs that relate to their feeling. Just a fun site to get a laugh with some vibrant and cartoonish design. Users can click on giphs to play or pause them.

A Golfer's Trivia

A Golfer's Trivia is a trivia game with golf-specific questions. Users will have ten seconds to answer the question and at the end results will be displayed. Even those golfing advocates may have a tough time with this one!
